IOT Based Waste Management Using Smart Dustbin

We have proposed a smart waste collection system on the basis of level of waste present in the dustbins. The data obtained through sensors attached to respective dustbin is transmitted over the server with the help of wifi module for storage. This stored data is then used for monitoring the daily selection of smart dustbins which are completely full and finding out their location with the help of GPS for deciding the optimal routes to pick several of the dustbins in minimal time, hence reduction in cost, resource optimization, effective usage of smart dustbins can be done.

A Self Care Band

Considering the covid scenario we have proposed a watch in which along with the basic requirements like time, date and day, we have added health monitoring sensors which will give temperature and heart rate of the user.We have also added a safe distance maintaining alarm which will help the user to maintain social distancing in crowdy places. There will be a sanitizer sprinkler attached itself to the watch along with a small keychain to avoid direct contact with other things.
