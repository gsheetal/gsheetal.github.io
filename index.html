IOT Based Waste Management Using Smart Dustbin

The real time waste management system by using smart dustbins to check the fill level of smart dustbins whether the dustbin are full or not. In this system the information of all smart dustbins can be accessed from Anywhere and anytime by the concern person and he/she can take a decision accordingly. By implementing this proposed system the cost reduction, resource optimization, effective usage of smart dustbins can be done. This system is indirectly reducing traffic in the city.

A Self Care Band

Considering the covid scenario we proposed a watch in which along with the basic requirements like time, date and day, we have added health monitoring sensors which will give temperature and heart rate of the user.We have also added a safe distance maintaining alarm which will help the user to mainatain social distancing in crowdy places. There will be a sanitizer sprinkler attached itself to the watch along with a small keychain to avoid direct contact with other things.
